class ContraceptionSwitchingPoll(module)[source]¶

Switching contraception status for population

This event looks across: 1) all women who are ‘not_using’ contraception to determine if they start using each method= according to initiation_rate1 (irate_1) 2) all women who are using a contraception method to determine if they switch to another method according to switching_rate, and then for those that switch directs towards a new method according to switching_matrix 3) all women who are using a contraception method to determine if they stop using it according to discontinuation_rate

(Was previously in Init1, Switch, Discontinue events)
