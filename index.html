Welcome¶

The Thanzi La Onse (TLO) Model is a part of the Thanzi La Onse (Health for All) Collaboration, funded by GCRF and UKRI in the United Kingdom.

It is developed in a collaboration between:

  • University of Malawi

  • Imperial College London

  • University College London

  • University of York

It benefits from a close partnership between this team and the Ministry of Health, Malawi.
