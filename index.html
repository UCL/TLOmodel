Welcome

The Thanzi La Onse (TLO) Model is a part of the Thanzi La Onse (Health for All) Collaboration, funded by Wellcome (and formerly GCRF and UKRI).

It is developed in a collaboration between:

  • Kamuzu University of Health Sciences

  • Imperial College London

  • University College London

  • University of York

  • East, Central and Southern Africa Health Community (ECSA-HC)

It benefits from a close partnership between this team and the Ministry of Health, Malawi.

Latest Release

The latest release of our code is available here and citable as:

The Thanzi La Onse Model. v0.1, Zenodo, 16 Nov. 2023, doi:10.5281/zenodo.10144016.

Open Source Code Repository

Our code base is open source and available for review and usage at https://github.com/UCL/TLOmodel
